Meet The Team 

We are Knight Dragon; entrepreneurial creative-spirited makers of places who bring new ideas and set new standards for urban living. We believe the power of creativity fosters socially and economically sustainable communities. Regeneration is about more than bricks and mortar.

We understand its many facets from the tangible to the nonmaterial, and harness the power of culture, art and design to create community.
